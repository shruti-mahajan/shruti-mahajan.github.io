Hey.

I'm Shruti Mahajan
Assistant Professor in the Computer Science department at Union College, NY
Research interests: Human Computer Interaction (HCI), User Experience (UX)
Check out my research group to learn more about our work: The Computers and People (CAP) Lab


I received my Ph.D. from the Human-Computer Interaction lab, Computer Science department at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI), MA in May 2024. My work has been published and recognized at top computing HCI conferences like the Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems (CHI) and the Conference on Computers and Accessibility (ASSETS) conferences. Across my research projects, I care deeply about conducting user-centered and participatory research with careful consideration of how technology impacts society.
Besides research, I actively engage mentoring and outreach efforts to promote inclusivity and diversity in the field of computing.
Outside work, I enjoy science fiction books/movies, and learning new languages!
